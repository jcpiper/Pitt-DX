Room Sponsorships

For $500 a room can be sponsored for 2 years. Rooms in the house are equipped with heat, but no air conditioning, so $300 of the fee will go towards purchasing an air conditioner for each room, which can be connected to the windows. The other $200 will go to the general House Improvement budget line, which finances repairs and other house projects. Each sponsor can choose which room in the house they would like to sponsor, and have a plaque with the name of their choice above the door. There are 14 occupied rooms in the house, sponsorships are first-come, first-serve.
Cost: $500
Status: Ongoing
Sponsors: Brock Stein - Sigma Class; Tom Radomski - Founding Father, Tim Pennington - Iota Class, Alex Vallejo - Rho Class, Clasy Hessler - Mu Class, Dr. Tim Corcoran
